CFGen - a Team Fortress 2 custom config generator

2.1.3

Please make a backup of your cfg and custom folders before using this if you want to save your old binds. This tool will overwrite all of your binds, and it is also recommended to be installed on a blank TF2, without any configs installed. You can wipe your configs by clearing your custom and cfg folders and launching TF2 with -autoconfig launch option. This tool is intended to be used with QWERTY keyboards, can work terrible if you have different layout.

    Damage dealt display:

    This section allows you to control damage numbers that appear when you deal a damage to your enemy.


    • Combat sounds:

    Here you can control sounds that are being played when you deal a damage.


    • Custom hitsounds and killsounds:

    There are already a few hitsounds that you can select from in an "Advanced Options" in-game menu. Optionally, you can select a custom hit/killsound (or choose none here and use your own one).
    Warning! The sound you selected will autoplay on click.

    • Connection settings:

    Equals to "good connection" by default. Don't touch if you don't understand what it is. If you are curious, here is a small article about connection settings. Very few servers can handle competitive connection so it is recommended to avoid using it.





    • Custom bindings:

    You may enter anything here, not just binds. Same formatting rules as for the TF2 console. This will be executed at the end of your config and overwrite existing binds.
